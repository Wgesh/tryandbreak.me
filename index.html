is both a technical demonstration and an interactive point and click game reminiscent of the early 90s.

How we made trytobreak.me (virtually) unbreakable.

There is nearly nothing you could do to stop trytobreak.me from running as intended. Perfectly responsive website aside, trytobreak.me also runs offline and with local styling sheets incase an unforeseen Y2K even happens. We also take away all but one way to access the inspect element panel to prevent you from messing up our already perfect website.
